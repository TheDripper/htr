Confronting Climate Change Together

The Haiti Takes Root Coalition is a dynamic coordination structure that facilitates information sharing, promotes learning about best practices and new innovations, and encourages collaboration for more effective activities to strengthen rural livelihoods, protect and renew the environment, manage natural resources and adapt to climate change.

Together for a Greener Haiti

Scroll to see Activities

Members of the Haiti Takes Root Coalition contribute to national environment and reforestation goals. While tree planting alone won’t ensure survival in the face of climate change, reforestation and re-greening are critical elements of an integrated response that encompasses environment, economy and energy.

Resilient Productive Landscapes

Resilient Productive Landscapes, or RPL, is a project of Haiti’s Ministry of Environment and Ministry of Agriculture, Rural Development and Natural Resources, with technical and financial support from the World Bank and J/P Haitian Relief Organization
